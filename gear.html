Razer Orochi V2 Wireless
Some consider this to rival the GPRO Superlight, an egg shaped, lightweight gaming mouse with a great sensor. And being affordable at the same time, though if you have bigger hands this may not be for you. It is a smaller mouse, but I have tested it and I have somewhat bigger hands, and it's shape feels great! The one downside I would say is the fact that it is not rechargeable, and you will need batteries. Though it does have a 950 hour battery life!

Logitech GPRO X Superlight Wireless
Considered to be the best gaming mouse you can get. The shape is a nice, almost an egg like feel, light weight and a great sensor. Also a huge plus to this mouse would be the fact that you can recharge it, no batteries needed! The only downside would be the price, but if you are looking for one of the best go for it!

Razer Viper Mini
A affordable mouse, it is light weight and has a nice shape to it. Compared to newer and more expensive mice, the sensor is not the best. But it is more than enough to get the job done!
